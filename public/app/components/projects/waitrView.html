WAITR

ABOUT

Wattr mobile app has two sides one for customers and the other for restaurant owners/employees. On the customers side it allows them to look at how long the wait is for a table, put their name down on the wait list, look at the menu, order food from the table, and pay on the app. On the restaurant side it lets the workers know who is next to get a table, which table placed a food order, and if the guest has paid for their bill. Since customers are ordering their own food it can eliminate miss communication with food orders. Overall this app will deliver quicker services, have a decrease in poor customer service, and is a convenient way of communicating at our fingertips.

TECHNOLOGY

The technology that waitr was built with was MongooseJS, Express.js, AngularJS, Node.js, Socket.io, Ionic, and Moment.js
