FORAGE

ABOUT

Forage has one signup page where users/owners can switch back and forth without having to make two separate accounts. This makes it convenient if someone would like to become a food truck owner or want to use the app to search for food trucks. Users can send private messages, order online, receive updates through text messages, and review different food trucks. Owners can turn on their location, add the menu, post hours of operation, and receive private messages. Forage makes it easier for food trucks to promote their business by customers seeing their location and being able to communicate with them through the website.
